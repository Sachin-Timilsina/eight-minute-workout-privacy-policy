Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 27, 2025

Thank you for using the 8 Minute Workout App. We take your privacy very seriously. This policy outlines what information the app handles and how it is protected. By using our app, you agree to this privacy policy.

1. Definitions

  • “We”, “us”, or “our” refers to the developer, Sachin Timilsina.
  • “You” refers to you, the individual using our Application.
  • “Application” or “Service” refers to the 8 Minute Workout App.
  • “Personal Data” refers to information you provide, such as your weight and height.

2. Information and Data Handling

Our application is designed to function entirely on your device. We do not collect or store your data on any external servers. The information we handle depends on how you use our services.

Information You Provide Voluntarily

When using the app, you can provide your height and weight. This information is used exclusively to calculate metrics like your BMI and the calories you burn during exercise. This data is stored locally on your device and can be updated by you at any time.

Information Generated During Use

The app locally saves your workout history (your “Activities”), including exercises completed, session duration, and calories burned. It also saves your app preferences, such as sound settings and exercise customizations, to improve your experience.

Feedback Information

If you choose to send us feedback or report a bug, your email client will be used. Information such as your app version and device model may be included to help us diagnose issues. This is voluntary, and this information is only collected if you decide to send it.

3. How We Use and Share Information

All information is used to provide you with a personalized and functional experience. We NEVER share the information handled by our app with any third parties. Since our app does not include advertisements, no data is shared with advertising partners.

4. Data Retention and Control

Your data is stored only on your device. If you uninstall the app, your data is permanently deleted. You can also delete all your data at any time by using the “Clear Data” option in the app’s settings.

5. Security

We take reasonable measures to safeguard the information stored within the app on your device. However, please note that no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

6. Children’s Privacy

We do not knowingly collect any personal data from individuals under the age of 13. If you believe a child has provided us with data, please contact us so we can take appropriate action.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the “Last Updated” date.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy, please contact us through the "Report Bugs" or "Suggest Features" options in the app or via email at sachintimilsina22@gmail.com.
